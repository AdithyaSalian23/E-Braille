About the Project

The E-Braille Learning Device is designed to help visually impaired individuals learn Braille effectively through interactive methods.

  • Three learning modes: Speak & Practice, Type & Practice, and Quiz Mode.
  • Integrated Braille keyboard and solenoid actuators.
  • Audio-assisted learning guidance.
  • Compact, cost-effective, and user-friendly design.
